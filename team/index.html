Team

Meet the team

Manmeet Poonam Sandhu, RN, BSN, BGS, MPH
President & Chief Executive Officer

For the last six years our President, Poonam Sandhu, has been working to green up outdoor events. Starting with the Vaisakhi parades in Vancouver and Surrey, Canada and now outdoor events in the DC, Maryland and Virginia region - Poonam is dedicated to waste diversion for the sake of human and environmental protection.

While working as a nurse, Poonam, along with hospital fellow staff, advocated the idea of bedside recycling at the hospital. Her work has gained the recognition of Vancouver City Council, the DC Mayor's office, and hundreds of thousands of event patrons, eventually landing her the recognition of Remarkable Women, 2010 (Vancouver) and Knapp Fellowship Award Recipient 2012.

Kranthi Kodali
Chief Operations Officer

The incentive of being "Green" involves lot of additional costs as it would require add-on services, increased effort and close monitoring. In the current struggling economies, this would be a very unattractive service to be hired.

Kranthi, with his background in business administration and engineering, adds significant value to the team and organization. He provides strategic solutions to improve operational efficiencies so the savings can be shared with our customers. Kranthi also overlooks the marketing and sales departments promoting the waste diversion services to broader markets and growing needs to solve some of the major environmental concerns.

Jingwen Wang
Product Manager

As the company's product manager, Jingwen is responsible for attending to numerous tasks, some of which include performing cost-benefit analysis for all event-related goods in an environmentally friendly manner, reporting on the profits and losses of events to our Chief Operations Officer, and identifying areas for improved efficiency and cost savings.

Jingwen is currently a graduate student at Johns Hopkins University, actively pursuing her Master of Science in Finance. She is set to graduate this year in December 2013.

Talent Manager

Raised on a wind-farm in Ohio, Austin was quite literally born into the "eco-aware" lifestyle. From a young age, the principles and values associated with recycling and composting have been committed to memory, influencing all aspects of his life. Since moving from Ohio to Washington, DC, He has spent the last two years pursuing his undergraduate degree in Industrial and Organizational Psychology at The George Washington University with an interest in sustainability.

With a focus on people, Austin has worked diligently to develop a talent pool of over 40 contracted workers from a diverse socio-economic background. Jonathan Chiu
Webmaster

Born and raised in New York City, Jonathan witnessed and still witnesses, first hand, the negative effects that carbon emissions and not recyling have on the environment. Countless experiences of walking through litter-ridden streets in the city has shown him the importance of being more green and environmentally conscious.

Jonathan is responsible for coding and maintaining everything that you see and experience on this website. He actively seeks to provide this company with a strong presence on the web that accurately represents the green services and presence that Waste Diversion LLC brings forth. He recently finished his first year at Northeastern University and is actively pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. In his spare time, Jonathan enjoys playing the guitar and coding. He is set to graduate in 2017.
