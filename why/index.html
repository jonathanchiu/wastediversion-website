Why

Why? We'll show you why

  Why us?

Right now, most events do not recycle. Understandably, event organizers are focused on making their event fun and exciting for visitors; trash is not their first priority. Trash is, however, our first priority, and keeping it away from the landfill makes us super happy!

We take care of all the work so that you can go back to focusing on making your event enjoyable for your attendees.

  Why run a green event?

Running a green event protects the health of humans and the environment by reducing the event's carbon footprint. Did you know that more than 54% of the trash thrown out in the country goes directly to landfills? (EPA.gov).

By choosing Waste Diversion LLC to green up your event, you and your event's participants play an active role in reducing the waste produced at events, which reduces the CO2 emissions, thus contributing to a sustainable environment for future generations.

See below for more information on protecting health, climate change and a carbon footprint.

  Why does it matter?

In 2006, scientists along with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention recognized that there was "a need to prepare for the inevitability of climate change, and the impact it would likely have on the health of US residents and the world population in general."

Some of the negative health effects of climate change include: increased aggravation of cardiovascular and respiratory disease, droughts which may concentrate contaminants in surface waters and may worsen the strain on those affected by food and hunger, and increased severity of allergies and asthma (CDC.gov).

  What is a carbon footprint?

Carbon footprint is determined by the amount of green house gases emitted by an organization, event, group, or person and is a measure of their environmental impact. You can calculate your event's carbon footprint and see your impact on the environment by utilizing the Environmental Protection Agency's official carbon footprint calculator. Check out The Simple Show's awesome video and website!

  What is global warming and climate change?

Global warming is the increase in the average temperature of the Earth's surface and its oceans as a result of the Earth's atmosphere trapping heat radiating from the Earth. Greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide and methane, rise up to the Earth's atmosphere and play a huge role in preventing heat from escaping.

This trapped heat has serious and negative climatic effects on our planet that could lead to a steady increase of its average temperature (a staggering 2.5 to 10 degrees Fahrenheit over the next century), the melting of glaciers which increases sea levels, more frequent heat waves, an increase in tropical cyclone activity, and greater chances of drought occurring globally.

For more information, check out the National Aeronautics and Space Administration's (NASA) websites on the evidence, causes, and effects of global warming and climate change. Check out National Geographic's informative video on global warming!

  What can you do?

Make an active decision to protect human health by reducing the waste produced at your event. Your choice will help us all fight against global warming and speak up for all the humans and animals in the world that are already impacted by climate change.

M.K Gandhi said it best, "Be the change you want to see in the world."
