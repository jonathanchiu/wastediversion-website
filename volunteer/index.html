Volunteer

Waste Diversion LLC aims to change the world through both an environmental and humanitarian effort. Our green team members are comprised of low to no income workers who may not be eligible for work elsewhere. Our Green Team Workers assist event patrons when it comes time to divert waste, With their help, Waste Diversion LLC is able to divert up to 60% more waste than free standing bins alone.

If you are interested in volunteering and helping us divert waste for events, please submit your information in the form below.
