About

We want to do great things for the world

A little about us

Waste Diversion LLC is a Clinton Global Initiative University 2012 project that has become a business. The business provides waste diversion services (recycling, composting, staffing, planning services, hauling, signs, bins, etc.) to outdoor events.

What we focus on

We are a triple bottom line company focusing on people, planet and profit.

People

Waste Diversion is committed to poverty alleviation, thus we only hire motivated local workers in the regions where we provide sevice (that lowers our carbon footprint too).

Planet

By coordinating green services and diverting waste for your event, we reduce carbon emissions that harm the planet. To learn more about a carbon footprint, click here.

Profit

We see profit not only in "dollars made," but in "dollars distributed" to protect the people of our communities and the environment we are working so hard to preserve.

Here are our goals

Our mission is to pioneer in providing effective on-site waste diversion services that preserve, improve and protect our environment and quality of life in the communities we serve. Our startup service has a medley of five themes:

  •  Public service - we will create jobs for low income and hard to employ individuals

  •  Environmental protection - we will provide the infrastructure and education needed to execute "greener" events in DC.

  •  Data generation - by partnering with our waste hauler, we will quantify the carbon savings impact of our waste diversion efforts for each event.

  •  Policy change - we are actively lobbying city council to update the Special Event Permit such that waste diversion at outdoor events is mandatory; similar to California, Oregon and Texas.

  •  Industry development - we will partner with local companies to provide a seamless waste diversion service for all incoming events. Waste Diversion believes in building the local economy
