Services

Before, during and after the event, we have a thorough service plan for you

Mix and match services

Waste Diversion LLC believes in providing you with thorough green services that you can mix and match to cater to your event's needs. Want us to set up waste stations, but have your own staff for grounds maintenance? No worries, you can do that.

We believe in providing you with a custom waste diversion plan with only the services you want. Choose as many or as little services that you want!

Before

  •  Creation of an "Event Waste Plan" focused on strategic bin placement
  •  Submission of your waste plan to the Special Event Permitting Office

During

  •  Bins and roll-offs for recycling, composting and/or garbage
  •  Set up of waste stations
  •  Staffing to manage the waste stations
  •  Staffing for grounds maintenance
  •  On-site supervisors to ensure the smooth operations of your event waste plan
  •  Organic cotton t-shirts that say, "We Lowered Our Carbon Footprint"

After

  •  Staffing for grounds clean-up after the event is over
  •  A "Diversion Certificate" identifying how much waste you saved from reaching the landfill
  •  An endorsement to the Environmental Protection Agency's WasteWise Program
